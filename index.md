## Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: May 2026**

Your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile Cafefinder application (the "App") handles location data and information related to your points of interest (POIs).

### 1. No Data Collection or Tracking
The developer does not track, store, harvest, or share your device's location. We do not operate external servers to collect your personal movement data, and we do not share or sell any location information to third-party advertising or analytics companies.

### 2. How Location Data is Used
The App requires access to your device’s location services strictly to provide core, on-device functionality:

* **Creating Points of Interest:** To help you easily log a waypoint or cafe at your current physical location.
* **Sorting and Distance Calculations:** To sort your saved lists of waypoints and show you how far away you are from a specific destination.

### 3. Local Storage
Any points of interest, custom notes, web addresses, or What3Words coordinates you save are stored locally on your device. You control your data.

### 4. Third-Party Navigation
When you launch navigation to a saved waypoint, the App passes the coordinates to your choice of native mapping applications (such as Apple Maps or Google Maps) installed on your device. Those external apps are governed by their own respective privacy policies.

### 5. Consent
By using the App, you consent to the on-device processing of your location data as described above. You can revoke location permissions at any time via your iOS Device Settings.
